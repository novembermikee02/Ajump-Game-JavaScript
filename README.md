<h5>AJUMP GAME</h5>

- Easy and intuitive controls.<br>
- music and old school sound effects.<br>

<h5>HOW TO PLAY</h5> 
- Use button to arrows button, top,left,right.<br>
- Jump on the top of monsters to defeat them.<br>
